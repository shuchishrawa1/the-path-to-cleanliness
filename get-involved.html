Contact Your Local Authorities

If your area is not clean and not improving, it is important to inform your local Nagar Nigam, Nagar Palika, Panchayat or city municipal corporation with clear details and photos.

You can:

  • Search for “[Your city] municipal corporation complaint” to find the official portal.
  • Use the Swachhata or city grievance app, if available, to upload photos and location.
  • Look for phone numbers or emails printed on local garbage vans or notice boards.

This section can be updated with specific emails, helplines or portals for your city or village as your NGO collects that information.

How We Use Your Data

We use your name and contact details only to coordinate activities, issue certificates and share project updates related to The Path to Cleanliness. We do not share your information outside the NGO without your consent.
